ABOUT US

APARTMENT is a Web-based apartment management solution that enables owners of residential properties to take complete control of every aspect of their business, including the rent, vacancy and maintenance cycles. Its streamlines management of the rent cycle with key features such as tenant and lease tracking, full general ledger accounting, automated rent and late fee reminders and on-demand reports. Additionally, rent collection can be automated; and property managers can receive online payments via Buildium’s tenant portal. Tenants and rental owners can submit requests online via its’s tenant and owner portals. Property managers can turn these requests into work items and assign them for maintenance.
